Ten Principles for Good Design

by Dieter Rams

Good Design is...

  1. Is innovative
  2. Makes a product useful
  4. Is aesthetic
  5. Makes a product understandable
  6. Is unobtrusive
  7. Is honest
  8. Is long-lasting
  9. Is thorough down to the last detail
  10. Is environmentally friendly
  11. Is as little design as possible
